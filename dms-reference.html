Features

Everything you need to manage
documents intelligently

From secure storage to automated workflows, DigiDocSmart delivers a complete document management ecosystem built for enterprise demands.

Document Management

Upload, organize, and manage documents with hierarchical folders, custom metadata, tags, and powerful full-text search. Support for bulk operations and drag-and-drop.

Enterprise Security

Classification-based access control, two-factor authentication, session fingerprinting, CSRF protection, password policies, and 256-bit encryption at rest and in transit.

Workflow Automation

Design multi-step approval workflows with templates, automatic routing, timeout handling, and complete audit trails. Streamline document reviews and approvals.

Collaboration & Sharing

Share documents internally or externally with granular permissions. Password-protected links, download limits, expiration dates, and real-time share analytics.

Version Control

Track every change with major/minor versioning, change notes, and instant rollback. File check-in/check-out prevents conflicts during collaborative editing.

Audit & Compliance

Tamper-proof audit trails with hash-chain integrity, geolocation tracking, device fingerprinting, and exportable logs. Meet regulatory compliance with confidence.

Advanced Search

Find any document instantly with full-text search, advanced filters, faceted navigation, saved searches, and search history. Filter by type, date, classification, tags, and more.

Dashboard & Analytics

Real-time dashboards with storage analytics, activity trends, user metrics, pending tasks, and system health monitoring. Make data-driven decisions effortlessly.

Multi-Storage Support

Store documents on AWS S3, local storage, SMB network shares, or NFS. Flexible storage backends let you choose what works for your infrastructure.

Role-Based Access

Six-tier role hierarchy with granular permission matrices. Control access at document, folder, department, and system levels with inheritance and explicit grants.

Backup & Recovery

Automated full and incremental backups with integrity verification, scheduling, and one-click restoration. Your documents are always safe and recoverable.

Notifications & Alerts

Stay informed with real-time notifications for shares, workflow updates, comments, and system events. Customizable preferences and email alerts keep your team in sync.
